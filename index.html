Terms & Conditions

Please read before placing an order.

🔒 Payment Policy

50% advance payment is required before starting the project. Remaining 50% is due upon project completion and before final delivery. All payments via UPI (shaikh.salik@fam) or as agreed.

🔄 Revision Policy

Each package includes up to 3 free revision rounds. Additional revisions beyond the free limit will be charged separately. Revisions must be requested within 7 days of delivery.

⏱️ Delivery Timeline

Basic (1 page): 2–4 working days. Standard (3–4 pages): 5–8 working days. Premium packages: 10–15 working days. Timelines may vary based on client feedback response time.

🌐 Domain & Hosting Renewal

Packages including domain and/or hosting cover the first year only. After 1 year, renewal charges apply separately as per the service provider's pricing at that time. Clients will be notified in advance before expiry.

📦 Ownership & Delivery

Full ownership of the website is transferred to the client upon final payment. Source code is provided on request. Hosting credentials are shared after full payment is received.

🚫 Cancellation Policy

If client cancels after work has started, the advance payment is non-refundable. If I am unable to deliver, full advance will be refunded. Mutual agreement may lead to alternative arrangements.

📞 Support

Post-delivery support for bug fixes is provided free for 15 days. After that, maintenance charges apply. Content updates, new features, or major redesigns are quoted separately.
